About

"Built with passion, served with warmth."

Sweet & Sip is a cozy little cake shop founded in 2025 by Dwi Febriyanti, a 21-year-old dessert enthusiast with a dream of bringing comfort and connection through every slice and sip.

Nestled in the heart of SCBD Jakarta, our shop blends minimalist Korean-inspired design with a warm, welcoming atmosphere — just like the cake cafés you’ve seen in your favorite dramas. More than just a place to eat, Sweet & Sip is a space where family isn’t always by blood, but by choice. Whether you're here to celebrate something sweet or simply unwind with a cup of coffee, we’re here to make you feel at home — one slice and one sip at a time.
